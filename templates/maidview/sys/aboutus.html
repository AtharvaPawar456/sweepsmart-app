About Us

Welcome to Sweep Smart, your trusted platform for seamless home service connections. We specialize in linking individuals with professional maids who provide essential home services like floor cleaning, dishwashing, and more.

Our goal is to simplify household management by offering a reliable, efficient, and user-friendly solution that caters to modern lifestyles.

With a secure email-based sign-in system, users can effortlessly create accounts and access our platform with ease. We prioritize safety, convenience, and trust, ensuring a smooth experience for both service seekers and providers.

Whether you are looking for help with daily chores or offering professional home services, Sweep Smart creates a reliable ecosystem where quality service and customer satisfaction come first.

Designed for efficiency and ease of use, our application streamlines the process of finding and booking maids, eliminating the hassle of traditional service hiring. By bridging the gap between home service providers and customers, Sweep Smart transforms the way people access household assistance, making life more convenient, one service at a time.
